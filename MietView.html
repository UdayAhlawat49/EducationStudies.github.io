MIET Highlights 2023

Meerut Institute of Engineering & Technology (MIET) is a prestigious institution Meerut offers a quality education at a higher level. Established in 1997, it is managed and governed by the City Educational & Social Welfare Society. The institute has an affiliation with the Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam University- AKTU (formerly Uttar Pradesh Technical University) and offers courses approved by the All India Council of Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi. NAAC has accredited it with a 'B++ Grade'. MIET also holds accreditation from the National Board of Accreditation (NBA).

MIET offers a total of 28 courses at the undergraduate and postgraduate levels. It extends courses in the streams of Engineering, Computer Applications, Management, Applied Science, Education, and Pharmacy. It also offers vocational courses in the disciplines of Engineering and Management. Besides this, it offers special training programmes such as AWS, SalesForce, IBM Blockchain, IBM Cyber Security, and SAP. MIET has two hostels for boys and one hostel for girls with capacities of 900 and 300 respectively. Other on-campus facilities include a library, a gymnasium, medical centre, a canteen, post office, PNB ATM, shop with stationery, photocopying, printing & binding facilities, faculty residence, and guest house. MIET Meerut Admission Process 2023

MIET Meerut admission procedure comprises merit in the qualifying examination or entrance exam scores. Meerut Institute of Engineering and Technology (MIET) offers 28 undergraduate and postgraduate programmes in Engineering & Technology, Applied Science, Pharmacy, Education, Computer Applications, and Management. The institute also offers vocational courses in Engineering and Management. Besides this, it offers additional training for adding value to the courses.

The mode of application for all the courses at MIET is online. MIET provides numerous placement opportunities to students in top-recruiting companies like Accenture, Genpact, Axis Bank, TCS, Tech Mahindra, and Cognizant Technologies.

MIET Meerut Admission 2023: Eligibility & Selection Criteria

Meerut Institute of Engineering and Technology offers several UG and PG courses, admission to which is granted basis the merit in the qualifying examination and scores in the entrance exam. It accepts the scores of various state and national level entrance exams like UPSEE, JEE Main, CAT, MAT, and others. The applicants must meet the basic eligibility for the desired courses before applying for the course.

MIET Meerut Admission 2023: How to Apply?

Candidates seeking admission to various courses at Meerut Institute of Engineering and Technology must submit an application form to be considered for the later process. To register online, follow the step-wise MIET application process demonstrated in the infographic below:

Documents required:

There are several documents that candidates need to submit during the application submission. Once the candidates are selected, they need to submit several other documents during admission.

List of documents required during the application process:

  • Class 10 and Class 12 mark sheets and passing certificates
  • Entrance exam scorecard
  • Passport-size photograph
  • Bachelor’s degree certificate (if applicable)

List of documents required during the admission process:

  • MIET Admission Form (Original and two photocopies)
  • Three set photocopies of Original Counseling Allotment Letter
  • Photocopy of UPSEE rank letter
  • Three set photocopies of Adhaar Card
  • Three set photocopies of PAN Card
  • Two attested copies of the GATE/GPAT/CAT/MAT score card (whichever applicable)
  • Three attested copies of Class 10 and Class 12 marks sheets and certificates
  • Migration certificate
  • Character certificate
  • Transfer certificate
