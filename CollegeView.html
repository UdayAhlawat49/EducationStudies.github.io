Ludhiana Group of Colleges Overview

The Ludhiana Network of Colleges, or LGC for short, is a famous academic institution that is part of North India's largest set of educational institutions. LGC is regarded not only as an institution, but also as a daring undertaking and academic initiative launched by some of the region's most famous philanthropists. The Ludhiana Group of Colleges, which is affiliated with the Punjab Technical University (PTU), Jalandhar, has been recognised by the AICTE in New Delhi as well as the Punjab Government. Additionally, the Ludhiana Group of Colleges' LGC Polytechnic is AICTE-accredited and associated with Chandigarh's Punjab State Board of Technical Education and Industrial Training.

Ludhiana Group of Colleges Overview

Walk-in Inquiries

Students or candidates who want to see admission to any department can walk into the admission cell situated on the campus and take the application form. The form can be submitted to the admission office and a nominal application fee of INR 1000 is to be paid while submitting the form which can be paid by credit/debit card, net banking, or through UPI as well if the candidate is shortlisted, they will be reached out via phone or email by the admission team. The candidate will be given an acknowledgment receipt upon successful acceptance of the application form.

Online Application

Since the onset of the corona pandemic, most of the admission process is now online, and candidate can submit their application via the official website and pay the application fee of INR 1000 via net banking, through credit/debit cards, or even UPI system as well. The list of needed documents should also be uploaded on the same site by the candidate. Once the application is sent the candidate would receive an acknowledgment through email and via SMS as well.

Ludhiana Group of Colleges Application Fees

The application fees are the same for all the student categories such as General (GEN), Scheduled Caste (SC), Scheduled Tribe (ST), Other Backward Classes (OBC) and Other Categories. The Ludhiana Group of Colleges application fee is Rs 1,000 for registration.
