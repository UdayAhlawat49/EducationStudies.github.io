Best Engineering Colleges in India for B.Tech Education

Compilation of 80 Top B.Tech Colleges in India

Being one of the most in demand courses in India, the engineering colleges in India are plenty. These colleges offer admission in various areas of engineering namely civil engineering, aeronautical engineering, mechanical engineering, electrical engineering etc. As it is one of the most sought after career options in India, the demand for individuals skilled in engineering is at an all time high with big shot multinational companies looking to hire technically adept employees. While there are almost 4200 B.Tech colleges in India, it is difficult to decide the best. Some of the popular states in India such as Tamil Nadu, Maharashtra, Andhra, Karnataka, Telangana, Uttar Pradesh, Delhi and Madhya Pradesh have a maximum number of engineering colleges (300+ in each state).
