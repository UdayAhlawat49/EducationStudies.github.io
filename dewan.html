Dewan V.S Group of Institutions Highlights 2023

Dewan V.S Group of Institutions is a Private college established in year 1996. The college is located in Meerut, Uttar Pradesh. Dewan V.S Group of Institutions is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education, Ministry of HRD, New Delhi, NCTE, NCHMCT, Ministry of Tourism, New Delhi and Bar Council of India, New Delhi. Moreover, the courses of the institute are also affiliated to Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University, Uttar Pradesh, Lucknow, CCS University, Meerut The institute offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and certificate programmes. The courses provided by the innstitute include B.Tech., BBA, BCA, B.Sc.(HHA), BHMCT, B.A.LL.B., B.Com.LL.B, LL.B., B.Ed., MCA, MBA, PGDM & LL.M. These Degree programmes are in the streams of Hospitality & Travel, IT & Software, Engineering, Business & Management Studies, Teaching & Education and Law. All these programmes are offered in Full Time mode. Below are the major highlights of Dewan V.S Group of Institutions:

MIET offers a total of 28 courses at the undergraduate and postgraduate levels. It extends courses in the streams of Engineering, Computer Applications, Management, Applied Science, Education, and Pharmacy. It also offers vocational courses in the disciplines of Engineering and Management. Besides this, it offers special training programmes such as AWS, SalesForce, IBM Blockchain, IBM Cyber Security, and SAP. MIET has two hostels for boys and one hostel for girls with capacities of 900 and 300 respectively. Other on-campus facilities include a library, a gymnasium, medical centre, a canteen, post office, PNB ATM, shop with stationery, photocopying, printing & binding facilities, faculty residence, and guest house. Dewan VS Group of Institutions India Admission 2023: How to Apply?

Candidates seeking admission to various courses at Meerut Institute of Engineering and Technology must submit an application form to be considered for the later process. To register online, follow the step-wise MIET application process demonstrated in the infographic below:

Documents required:

There are several documents that candidates need to submit during the application submission. Once the candidates are selected, they need to submit several other documents during admission.

List of documents required during the application process:

  • Class 10 and Class 12 mark sheets and passing certificates
  • Entrance exam scorecard
  • Passport-size photograph
  • Bachelor’s degree certificate (if applicable)

List of documents required during the admission process:

  • Dewan VS Group of Institutions India Admission Form (Original and two photocopies)
  • Three set photocopies of Original Counseling Allotment Letter
  • Photocopy of UPSEE rank letter
  • Three set photocopies of Adhaar Card
  • Three set photocopies of PAN Card
  • Two attested copies of the GATE/GPAT/CAT/MAT score card (whichever applicable)
  • Three attested copies of Class 10 and Class 12 marks sheets and certificates
  • Migration certificate
  • Character certificate
  • Transfer certificate
